Welcome to ccsit vadakara

CENTRE FOR COMPUTER SCIENCE AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (CCSIT) Vatakara previously known as MCA Centre Vatakara was established in 1998. This is one of the oldest regional centres directly run by the university of calicut. CCSIT is well known higher education institute in vatakara municipality.It is situated only 3 kms away from vatakara railway station and private bus stand.
